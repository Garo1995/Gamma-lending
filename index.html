What else do I need to know before starting?

Honestly, briefly, without hidden details — just how we like it

Do I need to install the program?

No, Gamma works directly in the browser. You don't need to download, install, or update anything. Just open the website and get started.

What happens to my data?

Your presentations and documents are stored in a secure cloud. You can delete them at any time. We do not share your data with third parties without your consent.

What languages does the service support?

Gamma supports 20+ languages, including Russian, English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and others. The AI can generate and translate content in any of these languages.

Can I use my own images?

Yes, you can upload your own images, logos, and fonts. Gamma will adjust the design to fit your brand.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, Gamma is only available in a web version, which works great on mobile devices through the browser. We are working on native apps for iOS and Android—stay tuned for updates.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time in your account settings. No penalties or hidden fees. You will continue to have access to paid features until the end of the paid period.
